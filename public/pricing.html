Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to what most agents ask before signing up.

Do I need any technical skills to set this up? +
None at all. We handle the full setup — connecting your Gmail, your lead sources, and your CRM. You just tell us where your leads come in from. Most agents are fully live within 24-48 hours.
What lead sources can LeadPilot connect to? +
Your own website forms, Zillow, Realtor.com, Facebook ads, Google ads, OpenHouse sign-in apps, and any source that can send a webhook or email notification. If you have a lead source we don't already integrate with, we'll add it during setup.
How is this different from a regular CRM or autoresponder? +
Templates feel like templates. LeadPilot uses Claude AI to write each email from scratch using the lead's actual budget, location, and property interest — so it reads like you sat down and typed it. Then it follows up with a different angle every 2 days until they reply.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Monthly plans cancel anytime. Yearly plans cancel at the end of your term. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no questions asked.
What happens to my leads if I cancel? +
They're yours. Every lead is stored in your own Google Sheet, in your own Google account. We don't keep them, and you can keep using the spreadsheet forever — even after you cancel.
Is there a free trial? +
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If LeadPilot isn't a fit in your first week, send us a WhatsApp message and we'll refund you in full — no forms, no friction.
Do you work with agents outside the US? +
Yes. LeadPilot works for agents in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, Dubai, and beyond. The AI writes in English by default; we can configure other languages on Plus and Pro plans.
